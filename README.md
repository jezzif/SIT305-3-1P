# SIT305 3.1C
A multiple choice quiz app!
## Features
- Real-time progress bar
- Instant answer feedback
- Results upon completion
- Retake the quiz
## Usage
Enter your name and start your quiz!   
Read through the question title and details carefully before selecting an answer, then press one of the three answer options. Make sure you double check you're correct, then select the submit button. You will receive instant feedback on whether you were right or wrong, the correct answer will turn green and if you selected an incorrect answer it will turn red. Once you have reviewed your answer hit next and move onto the next question. After all the questions have been completed your score will be shown to you and you may either take the quiz again or exit the app.
